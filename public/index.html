Quick start

No sketch? Pick a starter — Conjure builds a working app from a prompt while you watch.

Draw right on your app

Your app is live in the preview. Hit ✎ Markup on it, then draw your edits straight onto it — circle what to change, cross out (✗) what to delete, add an arrow, write a label. Colors carry meaning. Press Build and it rebuilds from your markup.

Brand-new project with nothing built yet? Just draw the UI you want — your first Build turns the drawing into the app.

Pages: hit + Page above the preview to add pages to this site. They get linked in the nav, and every edit applies to the page you're currently viewing (shown as “on: …”).

Point at your paper sketch. When the drawing changes and then holds still, Conjure commits it automatically.
