How it works

Three moves. That's the whole loop.

No syntax to learn and no prompt to perfect — you point, draw, and press build.

01

Draw on your app

Turn on Markup and sketch your edits straight onto the running app — circle it, cross it out to delete, fill it in to recolor, or write a note. Colors carry meaning.

02

Build it

Press Build and Conjure rebuilds from your markup — usually in seconds. Watch it think in the live terminal.

03

It's live

Your working app updates in the preview. Close the tab and come back — builds keep running on the server.

Features

Built for people who think in pictures.

Most tools want you to type. Conjure wants you to draw.

Markup-to-edit

Draw directly on the live app to change it. Circle, cross out, fill in, annotate — the app rebuilds from your marks.

Paper, via camera

Sketch on a napkin, point your camera, and Conjure captures it the moment your drawing holds still.

Multi-page sites

Add pages that link themselves into the nav. Every edit lands on the page you're actually looking at.

Fast edits

Small changes patch the file in place, so tweaks land in seconds instead of rebuilding everything.

Designed, not sloppy

An optimized design system is baked in — restrained palettes, real hierarchy, no generic "AI look".

Runs anywhere

One self-contained file per app. Nothing to deploy to try an idea — it's live in the preview instantly.
